# Nuroz

- Nuroz is your health app for well-being and compassion. Share your emotions with one of three emojis and specify why you feel that way to receive personalized help texts. If you're feeling down, send an uplifting message to other users that will be displayed to them. Experience appreciation and compassion while spreading positive emotions. The group function enables individual support in teams or classes by addressing unique issues. Earn karma points for your activities and track your achievements in the profile.
